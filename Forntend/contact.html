FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick Answers to Common Questions

When is the registration deadline?

Registration closes on March 10, 2024. However, some events may have different deadlines. Check individual event pages for specific information.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! You can participate in multiple events as long as the schedules don't overlap. We recommend checking the event schedule carefully.

Is there any registration fee?

Most events are free for college students. Some workshops and special events may have nominal fees. All details are provided on individual event pages.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your college ID card, registration confirmation, and any specific materials mentioned in the event requirements. Laptops may be needed for technical events.

Can external students participate?

Yes! External students are welcome to participate in most events. Some events may be exclusive to our college students - please check event details.

How do I become a volunteer?

Contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@college.edu or fill out the volunteer form available on our registration page.
